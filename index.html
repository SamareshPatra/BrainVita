How to play this game:
Step1: Click on a ball
Step2: Move 2 steps towards up, down, left or right and place the ball into any empty cell
Step3: Select next ball and follow step 1 and 2

Rule1: Can only move 2 steps at a time, neither 1 step or more than 2 steps
Rule2: Can not move in cross direction, only straight
Note: As less no of balls will left at end, as more talented you are.
